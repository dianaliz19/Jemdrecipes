RASPBERRY CHOCOLATE CHIP BROWNIE



METHOD


  1. In a medium saucepan, melt 1 1/2c bittersweet chocolate chips and butter. Stir until smooth. Remove from heat and stir in brown sugar, eggs, and vanilla. Beat until smooth. Stir in salt. Fold in flour, raspberries, and 1c semi-sweet (or bittersweet) chocolate chips until flour is just barely combined. Batter will be thick.

  2. Pour batter into an 8×8 baking dish lined with parchment paper. Smooth surface.

  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 25-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out with a few moist chocolate crumbs. Brownies may not seem all the way done. This is okay! They’ll finish setting as the brownies cool.

  4. Allow brownies to cool completely before cutting and serving. Store leftover brownies in the refrigerator.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 1/2c bittersweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2c butter
  • 1/2c brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1c flour
  • 1c raspberries (if using frozen, do not thaw)
  • 1c semi-sweet or bittersweet chocolate chips
